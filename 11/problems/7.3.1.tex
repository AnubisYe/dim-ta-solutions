\problem{1}
Suppose that $E$ and $F$ are events in a sample space and $p(E) = 1/3$, $p(F) = 1/2$, and $p(E|F) = 2/5$. Find $p(F|E)$.

\solution

Use Bayes' Theorem to get

\[ p(F|E) = \frac{p(E|F)\cdot p(F)}{p(E)} = \frac{2/5 \cdot 1/2}{1/3} = \frac{3}{5} \]

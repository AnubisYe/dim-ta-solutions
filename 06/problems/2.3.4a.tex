\problem{4}
Given
$A^{-1} = 
\left[\begin{array}{rrr}
	1 & -1 & 3 \\
	2 & 0 & 5 \\
	-1 & 1 & 0
\end{array}\right]$
:
\begin{enumerate}[a)]
	\item Solve the system of equations
		$
			AX =
			\left[\begin{array}{rrr}
				1 \\
				-1 \\
				3
			\end{array}\right]
		$.
\end{enumerate}

\solution

Multiply the inverse on both sides from the left to get

\[
    X = 
        \left[\begin{array}{rrr}
            1 & -1 & 3 \\
            2 & 0 & 5 \\
            -1 & 1 & 0
        \end{array}\right]
        \left[\begin{array}{rrr}
            1 \\
            -1 \\
            3
        \end{array}\right]
    =
        \left[\begin{array}{r}
            1+1+9\\
            2+0+15\\
            -1-1+0
        \end{array}\right]
    =
        \left[\begin{array}{r}
            11\\
            17\\
            -2
        \end{array}\right]
    \]

\problem{18}
(\emph{Modified from book.})
Let $L$ denote the line through the origin in $\RR^2$ with slope $m$.
The reflection in $L$ has matrix
\[
	A
	=
	\frac{1}{1 + m^2}
	\begin{bmatrix}
		1 - m^2 & 2m \\
		2m & m^2 - 1
	\end{bmatrix}.
\]
Show that $m = \sin\theta/\cos\theta = \tan\theta$ yields the formula of Example 10.
\solution

\problem{13}
What is the row of Pascal's triangle containing the binomial coefficients $\binom{9}{k}$, $0 \leq k \leq 9$?

\solution
\[
	\begin{matrix}
		\binom{9}{0}
		&
		\binom{9}{1}
		&
		\binom{9}{2}
		&
		\binom{9}{3}
		&
		\binom{9}{4}
		&
		\binom{9}{5}
		&
		\binom{9}{6}
		&
		\binom{9}{7}
		&
		\binom{9}{8}
		&
		\binom{9}{9}
	\end{matrix}
\]
or
\[
	\begin{matrix}1&9&36&84&126&126&84&36&9&1\end{matrix}
\]
